🛡️ Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being fully transparent about how your data is handled.

📅 Effective: June 8, 2026 🔄 Last Updated: June 8, 2026 📦 EventPay Management
📋 Quick Summary: EventPay collects only the information needed to process your event contribution — your name, village/area, phone number, and UPI transaction details. We never sell your data to third parties.
1

📂 Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information required to manage event contributions securely:

Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
IdentityFull name, phone numberIdentify contributor & communicate
LocationVillage / Area nameAnalytics & organizer reporting
PaymentContribution amount, UTR/Transaction IDVerify and track payment
ComplaintEmail, description, optional file attachmentResolve issues raised by users
TechnicalReference ID generated at submissionTrack status & avoid duplicates

We do not collect passwords, bank account details, device fingerprints, or any biometric data.

2

⚙️ How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for event management purposes:

  • To record and verify your payment contribution for the event
  • To generate a digital receipt with your Reference ID
  • To display aggregate statistics to the organizer (total collected, village breakdown)
  • To allow you to check your payment status using your phone number or Ref ID
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or duplicate submissions
  • To respond to complaints or support requests you submit
✅ We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, marketing companies, or any unrelated third parties.
3

💳 Payment Information

EventPay uses a UPI-based payment workflow. Here's exactly what we do and don't store:

  • We record your UTR / Transaction ID (the 12-digit reference from your payment app) to verify the transfer
  • We record the amount you entered and the status (Pending / Verified / Not Verified)
  • We do not store your UPI PIN, bank account number, IFSC, or card details
  • Actual money transfer happens directly via your UPI app — EventPay only records the result
⚠️ Important: If you use PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, or BHIM to make the payment, those apps have their own privacy policies which govern the transaction on their end.
4

🤝 Data Sharing & Disclosure

Your data is not sold or traded. It may be accessed only in these limited circumstances:

  • Event Organizer: The authorized admin can view contribution records for the specific event you contributed to
  • Google Sheets / Drive: Data is stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet owned by the event organizer — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Fraud Prevention: Suspicious submissions may be flagged and reviewed by the event organizer
5

🔒 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Admin access is protected by password authentication and session tokens with automatic expiry
  • The underlying Google Sheets database is accessible only to authorized admin accounts
  • Fraud detection algorithms automatically flag suspicious UTR patterns before storage
🛡️ Note: While we apply strong practices, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Treat your Reference ID and phone number as private information.
6

✋ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information (e.g. wrong name or phone)
  • Deletion: Request removal of your record after the event concludes
  • Status Check: Check your payment status at any time using your Ref ID or phone number at status.html

To exercise these rights, contact us at likithmuddada@gmail.com. We respond within 7 business days.

7

🍪 Local Storage & Cookies

This app uses browser local storage (not cookies) for the following purposes only:

  • Theme preference: We remember whether you prefer Dark or Light mode
  • Admin session: A temporary session token is stored to keep you logged in as admin (expires per your configured timeout)

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site trackers, or analytics platforms. You can clear local storage any time in your browser settings — this will log out any active admin session and reset your theme.

8

🗓️ Data Retention

Payment and complaint records are retained in the event organizer's Google Sheet for the duration of the event and up to 1 year afterward for accounting purposes. After that period, the organizer is responsible for data deletion per their own policies.

9

👶 Children's Privacy

EventPay is not directed at users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app features change or legal requirements evolve. The "Last Updated" date above will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
